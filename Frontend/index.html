Where Artistry Meets Blockchain

PVA Bazaar is a decentralized marketplace connecting artisans with collectors, using blockchain technology to ensure provenance, authenticity, and fair compensation.

Our Ecosystem

Digital Provenance

Every physical item has a digital twin on the blockchain, ensuring authenticity and tracking its complete history from creation to current owner.

Fair Compensation

Artisans receive 50% of all sales directly to their wallets with transparent revenue sharing and automatic royalties from secondary market sales.

Global Marketplace

Connect with artisans and collectors from around the world in a decentralized ecosystem that transcends borders and traditional limitations.
